About Us

Local Knowledge,
Genuine Fun

Captain Zach has been fishing these waters since he was three years old, learning the ropes from his dad. That passion never left — these days you’ll find him on the Cape all summer and chasing fish in Hawaii every winter. He’s USCG-licensed and has spent his whole life on the water, and it shows. Now joined by his deckhand Sophia, a true lover of the ocean and everything that comes with it. Between the two of them, you’re in good hands — and guaranteed a great time.​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​

Whether it's your kids' first catch, a sunset toast with friends, or an offshore whale sighting you'll never forget, we keep trips personal, relaxed, and just the right amount of adventurous.
